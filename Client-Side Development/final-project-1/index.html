Purpose

Around the world, there have been a problem regarding waste management and dealing with recycling waste and garbage. The problem lies on people all around the world not understanding the purpose of recycling as they would place garbage into recycling and vice versa without realizing the unintended consequences. Additionally, the landfills of garbage waste are being piled up quicker than ever, causing the lack of space for waste. Through this website, we hope to inform the citizens of the Earth to take garbage management seriously and avoid creating waste.

Line graph depicts the high growth of waste generated in the United States throughout 2000 to 2013. The data is based on the amount of solid waste generated, calculated in pounds per person per day. This means that this data adapts as the population grows and it emphasizes the trend of increasing waste generated per person. As a result, as the population grows and this rate of waste generated per person grows, the United States and the World will soon have issues with disposing waste and landfills will continue to grow at an exponential rate, depleting land and popluting the Earth.
